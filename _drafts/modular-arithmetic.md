## Theorem 1: $3 \cong 1 (\text{mod } 2)$

### Proof
By definition:
$$
  3 \cong 1 (\text{mod } 2) \\
  \iff 2 \mid 3 - 1 \\
  \iff 2 \mid 2 \\
  \textit{trivial}
$$
